Capabilities

Natural Language Control

Describe tasks in plain English. No selectors, no scripting — just tell BlitzAgent what you want and it figures out the rest.

📝

Autonomous Form Filling

Automatically fills inputs, selects dropdowns, checks boxes, and submits forms across any website without manual intervention.

Screenshot Evidence

Captures screenshots at each step, giving you a complete visual audit trail of every action the agent took.
